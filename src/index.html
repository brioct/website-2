Common Health Insurance Myths

"I don't need it" - You may not "need" it now, but life is unpredictable. You don't want to wait for an accident to be covered!

"It's too expensive" - it depends, you can't compare it to your car insurance rate. Coverage doesn't have to be expensive, the earlier you get it, the less expensive it will be.

"I already have a plan" - When was the last time you spoke to your agent and went over your plan? Do you know what it covers, and if you needed surgery tomorrow would you know your max out of pocket?
